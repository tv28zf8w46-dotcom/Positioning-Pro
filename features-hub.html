Positioning Pro — Feature Previews

Standalone prototypes built from the design roadmap. Each is self-contained; click to open. Logic modules ship with passing self-tests and drop into the app with a line or two.

#1 · Flagship

3D Exam Room — SID & Detector

Orbitable WebGL room. Pick the receptor (Tabletop / Table Bucky / Wall Bucky) and dial the SID in inches; the tube auto-orients (swings horizontal for wall) and centering/angle come from the 2D activity. Green when receptor + SID match the order.

 #4 · Pedagogy

Simulated Radiograph

Renders the resulting film from your technique: under/over-exposure, low contrast, CR clipping, angle distortion — with a live fault list.

 #7 · Feel

Generator Console

Skeuomorphic panel: rotary kVp/mAs/mA/SID knobs, LED displays, APR presets, and a two-stage PREP→EXPOSE with Web-Audio capacitor whine.

 #8 · Theater

Cinematic Exposure

Lights dim, collimator light snaps on, marker glints, beam flash, then the radiograph develops in on the wall monitor.

 #13 · #16 · #17

Trauma Shift

Timed shift with hospital requisition cards (patient, history, priority), streak/combo multipliers, edge glow and score popups.

 #19 · Retention

Spaced-Repetition Review

SM-2 scheduler: weak projections resurface sooner, mastered ones stretch out. Time-travel through days and watch the due queue + skill map.

 #9 · Hardest to teach

Skull Positioning Lines

Pitch, rotate & tilt a 3D skull to align OML / IOML / GAL (sagittal lines) plus the MSP plane and IPL to the receptor. Live angle readout + pass check — near-impossible to grasp from a textbook.

 #14 · Progress

Progress Constellation

A skill skeleton instead of a stats table: each region glows by mastery. Click to drill into a region's projections. Driven by the review scheduler.

 #18 · Feedback

Attending Review

Replays a SIM step-by-step with an attending radiologist explaining each call and its radiographic consequence, then a scored verdict.

 #10 · Feel

Sound & Haptics

Web-Audio tones and vibration patterns for correct / wrong / milestone / knob / expose events. Drops into any interaction with one call.

 #3 · Shader

Volumetric Beam

GLSL fragment-shader x-ray cone: travelling scanlines, fresnel edge falloff, drifting dust, collimation borders that turn green within limits.

 #5 · AR

AR Mode

WebXR immersive-AR when the device supports it; otherwise a camera + device-orientation overlay projects the tube/CR onto the real world.

 #11 · Identity

Design System

Living style guide for design-tokens.css: slate+cyan palette, 8-pt spacing, minor-third type scale, three radii, one accent gradient.

 #12 · Motion

View Transitions

picker → room → review morph via the View Transitions API with shared-element badges, and a clean fade fallback where unsupported.

 #15 · Atmosphere

Suite Ambiance

Flickering wall viewboxes, safelight glow, dust drifting through the beam, reflective floor — depth with no performance cost. Pure CSS + canvas.
